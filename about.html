About Us

Our Story

Pearlcravino was founded with a simple mission: to bring the goodness of traditional Indian superfoods to modern consumers in a convenient and delicious form.

Our journey began when our founder discovered the incredible nutritional benefits of makhana (fox nuts) and realized that this ancient superfood deserved more recognition in today's health-conscious world.

Starting from a small kitchen experiment, we have grown into a brand that's trusted by thousands of families across India for providing premium quality makhana snacks that are both nutritious and delicious.

At Pearlcravino, we believe that healthy eating shouldn't mean compromising on taste. That's why we've perfected our recipes to create makhana snacks that are not just good for you, but also incredibly tasty.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients. We source the finest fox nuts and use only natural seasonings to create our products.

Health

We are committed to promoting healthier snacking habits by offering nutritious alternatives to traditional snacks.

Community

We support local farmers and communities by sourcing our raw materials directly from them, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices.

Sustainability

We are conscious of our environmental impact and strive to use eco-friendly packaging and sustainable production methods.
