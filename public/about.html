About Us

Our journey to bring you the finest makhana snacks

Our Story

Pearlcravino was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to transform the humble makhana (fox nut) into delicious, healthy snacks that everyone can enjoy.

Our journey began when our founder, Priya Sharma, discovered the nutritional benefits of makhana during her quest for healthy snacking options. Impressed by its protein content and low calorie count, she started experimenting with different flavors in her kitchen.

What started as a home-based venture quickly grew into a beloved brand, as more people discovered the delicious taste and health benefits of our premium makhana snacks.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, nutritious snacking options that support a healthy lifestyle without compromising on taste.

Our Vision

To become the leading brand for premium makhana snacks globally, known for quality, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients or production processes.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable sourcing and eco-friendly packaging.

Health

We believe that healthy snacks should be delicious and accessible to everyone.

Community

We support the communities where our ingredients are grown and processed.

Our Journey

2020

Pearlcravino was founded in Mumbai, starting with just three flavors of makhana snacks.

2021

Expanded our product line to include six unique flavors and began selling online across India.

2022

Opened our first dedicated production facility in Bihar, creating jobs for local communities.

2023

Launched eco-friendly packaging and began exporting to select international markets.

2024

Recognized as one of India's fastest-growing health food brands with nationwide distribution.

2025

Continuing our mission to bring premium makhana snacks to health-conscious consumers worldwide.
