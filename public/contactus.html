Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Pearlcravino products?

Pearlcravino products are available at select retail stores across major cities in India. You can also purchase them online through our website or popular e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and BigBasket.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select international locations. Please contact our customer service team for more information about international shipping options and rates.

Are your products suitable for vegans?

Most of our products are vegan-friendly. However, some flavors like our Caramel Makhana contain ghee (clarified butter). Please check the ingredients list on the product packaging or our website for specific information.

How long do your products stay fresh?

Our makhana snacks have a shelf life of 6 months from the date of manufacturing when stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, we recommend consuming them within 2 weeks for the best taste and texture.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk purchasing options?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@pearlcravino.com for more information and pricing details.
